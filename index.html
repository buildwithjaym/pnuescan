What we do

PneuScan AI adds a predictive layer to routine chest X-rays — helping health systems identify elevated-risk patients earlier and prioritize follow-up screening.

Predict, don’t diagnose

We estimate short-term risk (1–3 years) to support screening decisions — not to confirm cancer.

Opportunistic screening

Uses X-rays that already happen daily (ER, checkups, clearances) — no extra scan required.

Clinical workflow fit

Outputs a structured report and explainability overlay that can be reviewed by professionals.

Key features

Designed for adoption: fast, interpretable, and built around real clinical workflows.
